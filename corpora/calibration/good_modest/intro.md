# Field Notes — Introduction

These field notes describe how the support desk handles tickets. A **ticket** is a single
customer request. Tickets are grouped into **queues**. The vocabulary is consistent: ticket,
queue, agent.

## Basics

- **Ticket** — one customer request.
- **Queue** — a group of related tickets.
- **Agent** — the person who resolves a ticket.
