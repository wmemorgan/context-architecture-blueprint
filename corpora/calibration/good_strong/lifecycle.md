# Helix Knowledge Base — Record Lifecycle

A record moves through draft, review, and published states. The owner is accountable at each
state. Terminology matches the overview: catalog, record, owner. A published record always
has a source and a current version.

## States

- **Draft** — owner is writing the record.
- **Review** — a second owner checks the source and accuracy.
- **Published** — the record is live in its catalog with a cited source.
