# Helix Knowledge Base — Governance

Every record in a catalog cites its source and names its owner, so any claim can be traced.
Records are versioned: when a record changes, its version increments and the prior version
is retained for audit. Catalogs are reviewed quarterly.

## Provenance

Each record carries a source and an owner. Claims without a cited source are not published.
