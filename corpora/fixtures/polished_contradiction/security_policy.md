# Security Policy

## Scope
Per the Governance Office, this policy governs all tenants.

## Encryption
According to the standard, all customer data at rest is encrypted with AES-256.

## Retention
Customer records are retained for exactly 30 days, then purged.

## Access
Access is role-based and logged. See the access runbook for details.
